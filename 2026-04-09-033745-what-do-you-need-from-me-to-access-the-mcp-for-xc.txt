  Request the Family Controls Entitlement

  Link: https://developer.apple.com/contact/request/family-controls-distribution

  Steps

  1. Sign in to the Apple Developer account as the Account Holder (not just any
  team member — it must be the Account Holder role)
  2. Go to the request form at the link above
  3. Fill out the form — Apple asks:
    - Your app name and bundle ID (andyjphu.mathblocker)
    - A description of how your app uses Screen Time APIs
    - Which frameworks you'll use (FamilyControls, DeviceActivity,
  ManagedSettings, ManagedSettingsUI)
    - Whether you're using .individual or .family authorization (you want
  individual)
  4. Submit and wait — Apple reviews and grants the entitlement. Turnaround
  varies (days to weeks).
  5. Once approved, go to https://developer.apple.com/account → Identifiers →
  select your App ID → Additional Capabilities → enable Family Controls
  6. In Xcode: Target → Signing & Capabilities → + Capability → Family Controls
  7. Repeat steps 5-6 for each extension target's bundle ID (you'll need 3 more:
   monitor, shield config, shield action)
